[PEARLY GATES] 

1
Pearly gates, City of Light,
And descending from above,
Built for the Bride of the Lamb, 
Just the same eternally.

[Chorus]
Love is shining in that City,
Coming from the Lamb of God,
He the pearly gates will open,
In the New Jerusalem. 

2
Like a pilgrim heavy laden, 
Weak and helpless so was I; 
Brokenhearted, yet He healed me 
He will hear the sinner’s cry. 

3
Love divine, so great and wondrous, 
At His door I’ll knock and wait! 
I will sing His praise forever, 
For His blood, His power to save. 

4
Oh! What joy when Jesus comes back,
All our sorrows will be over, 
By His precious love to me,
I shall enter Heaven’s gate.
