[Chakutumaini Sina]

CHAKUTUMAINI SINA 
Ila damu yake Bwana 
Sina wema wa kutosha 
Zambi zangu kuziosha

"My hope is built on nothing less than Jesus’ 
blood and righteousness;
I’m not good enough to wash away my sins."

2
NJIA YANGU IWE NDEFU 
Yeye hunipa wokovu 
Mawimbi yakinipiga

"In my long journey He remains my Salvation;
When the storms hit,His strength is my anchor."

3
DAMU YAKE NA SADAKA
Nategemea daima

Mwokozi atanitosha

"His blood and promises support me in problems.
When all is done with here, the Saviour will rescue me."

4
NIKIITWA HUKUMUNI 
Rohoni nina amani

Sina hofu mbele yake

"When I am called in for judgement, I remain peaceful in my heart
since I’m dressed in His righteousness, faultless to stand before the throne."
